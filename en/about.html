About

Who am I?

My name is Léo Baudry and I'm a first-year student on the MMI course at the IUT in Montbéliard . I created this website as part of an individual project for my course.

What is the goal of my website?

My website aims to show its users, through articles and illustrations, the consequences of a future world devoid of technology.

At the same time, the site highlights the extent to which we are all far too dependent on technology, and provides ideas on how to break away from it (and presents the benefits).

Why choose this subject?

It's a very interesting and revealing subject to show the extent to which our dependence on technology is a central issue today.

Through various articles, it allows me to explore ideas of resilience in the face of current technological excesses.

My site offers an opportunity to reflect on our own consumption of technology, and helps us to take a step back from the subject.
